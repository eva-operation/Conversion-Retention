# Eva Staff: Search & Navigation Guide

This guide outlines how Eva Internal and Admin users can manage account contexts, switch views, and perform cross-module searches.

## 1. Switching to Eva Internal (Dahili Görünüm)
There are two primary ways to return to the internal dashboard from any account view:

### A. Sidebar Profile Action (Bottom Left)
- **Visual Indicator:** The profile section in the bottom left shows the current account's initials (e.g., "JB") or "EVA" when in the internal view.
- **Action:** When an account is selected, simply **click the profile area**. 
- **Feedback:** On hover, the initials will swap to a **LogOut icon**, and a tooltip will confirm "Quick Switch to Eva Internal". Clicking this immediately deselects the account and returns you to the internal team view.

### B. Global Search Dropdown Card
- **Action:** Click into the **Global Search bar** in the header.
- **Selection:** Click the purple **"Eva Internal"** card in the dropdown search view.
- **Purpose:** Quick context reset while already utilizing the search interface.

---

## 2. Searching & Selecting Accounts
Account selection is unified within the **Global Search** bar.

- **Action:** Type any term (Name, Store URL, Project Name, or Contact) into the header search bar.
- **Search Categories:**
    - **By Name:** Find brands or clients by name.
    - **By Project:** Match internal project codes.
    - **By Store:** Search via Store names or Shopify URLs.
    - **By Contact:** Match by email or contact person.
- **Selection:** Click any account in the results list to switch to that account's specific data view.

---

## 3. Contextual Searching (Within an Account)
When an account is selected, the Global Search bar provides deep-links into specific modules for that account.

### Module Deep-Links
Type in the search bar to see direct links for:
1.  **Search in Products:** Filters Analytics > Products for the account.
2.  **Search in Advertising Overview:** Filters Analytics > Advertising for the account.
3.  **Search in Tasks:** Filters the Tasks module for matching entries.

### Quick Account Switching (Switch to Another Account)
To move to a different account while searching within the current one:
1.  Click the **"Switch to Another Account"** card in the dropdown.
2.  The list of **Other Matching Accounts** will expand below.
3.  Click the desired account to switch context immediately.

---

## Design Standards: Visual Cues
- **Blue Icons/Cards:** Focus on switching or managing customer accounts.
- **Purple Icons/Cards:** Focus on Eva Internal/Admin administrative tools.
- **Gray Icons:** Indicate contextual search within the current account.

> [!TIP]
> Use **`⌘ + K`** (or `Ctrl + K`) to focus the Global Search bar instantly from any view.
